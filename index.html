रहता है एक ही कमरे में आदमी, उसका गुरूर रहता है, बाकी मकान में।
"A person lives in just one room,
His pride occupies the rest of the house."
आदमी
(Person)
गुरूर का स्थान
(Space of Pride)

Reflection on Humility

This profound couplet reminds us that while we physically occupy minimal space in this world, our ego and pride demand vast territories in our minds and relationships. True wisdom lies in recognizing this disproportion and choosing humility over the expansive hunger of our pride.

~ A timeless wisdom on the nature of human pride and humility ~
